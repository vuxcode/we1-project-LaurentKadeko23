Preperation

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen in water helps flavour!
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug {always} a big mug.
  3. Pur the how water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea need time to unlock all its flavour, so give it 3-4 minutes to do its thing. This is a perfect time to grab a seany cookie or day dream about vacation.
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squig it wih a spoon against the side of the mug. Not to much or You'll make it better.
  6. If you want, throw in som emilg or sugar honey or nothing else at all
  7. Enjoy!
